Clever Oven

Website - Python/HTML/CSS/JS/PHP - Teamwork - School

Clever Oven was a one year project realised during my last highschool year. The goal was to create a website for visually impaired people who struggle with cooking. Indeed, for many pre-prepared food, guidelines are written with a very small font. The website helps them with this everyday dutty by automatically setting their oven for a specific aliment saved on their account. The management of registred users' information uses python and php programs with the most sensitive information encrypted.

The Snake Game

Video Game - C89 - Teamwork - School

The Snake Game was one of my firsts projects during my two years degree. The instructions are very simple: the player controls a constantly moving snake which collects pellets in order to gain points and, at the same time, grow. The project must contain a snake game with a few more rules including a timer, a button to pause or exit the game and a score system. For the graphic aspect of the game, I use a rudimentary graphic library given by the university.
